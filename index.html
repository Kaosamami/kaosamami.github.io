Weezy: his Royal Fluffiness

The biography of Weezy the 1st

Life

Weezy was born sometime around June 2011 in the south-west of France. As a young kitten, his favorite pastime was to destroy the plants of the neighbor and play with his brother. After being adopted by his new family (soon-to-be servants), the first thing he did was falling into the fireplace vent and gave them quite the scare. But he quickly found his marks and started enjoying his new pastime: bitting feet.

As a 11 year-old fluffy cat, Weezy enjoy his days comfortably with his servants to answer his every need. He starts every day with a petting session and a patrol of his territory. Then, he goes back to sleep until dinner time. Sometimes, he'll bring back a bird of a mouse because his hoomans are "very bad at hutting".
